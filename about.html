Donate Blood, Save Lives.

Red Aid, founded by Mila Malaeb, is a pioneering Lebanese startup with a clear mission: to simplify and revolutionize blood donation in Lebanon. As a country often grappling with medical supply shortages, Mila recognized the need for an efficient platform to bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients.

A small act of kindness can be the lifeline someone needs today.

Starting with a vision of kindness and community, Red Aid launched a user-friendly platform to make donating blood as simple as a few clicks. The platform connects donors to those in urgent need, empowering people to play a vital role in saving lives. Mila's leadership and her team's dedication have created a movement where every Lebanese citizen can contribute to building a healthier, stronger society. Join us in our mission to ensure that no one has to wait for life-saving blood. Together, we can make a difference, one donation at a time.

About Our Mission

We are dedicated to connecting blood donors with those in need, fostering a culture of giving, and saving lives every day. Together, we can make a difference!
